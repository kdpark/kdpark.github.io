Bio

Kyudong Park is a doctoral student at the Dept. of Creative IT Engineering of POSTECH (Pohang University of Science and Technology) in South Korea. His research interests center on Human-Computer Interaction for understanding what people want and what technology can deliver. He has joined Experience Design & Engineering Lab. (Prof. Sung H. Han), and his recent research focuses on Human factors and User Experience (UX) in Information Technology.
